Features

Turn by turn guidance to popular points of interest
Want direction to your check-in counter? Just tap and start walking! Kids whining in hunger? Tap any of the available restaurants or cafes to see where exactly to go!
Easy to use interface and smart guidance
A few taps launch you straight into the app with minimal hussle. By giving your flight number, you can easily be guided to the check-in counters and departure gates for your specific flight.
Aloha! Salaam! Bienvenido!
See everything in the app in YOUR favourite language to feel familiar to the environment. We have initial support for a limited set, but more to come in the future hopefully!
