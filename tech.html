Vuforia

Multi-target detection, target tracking, virtual buttons, Smart TerrainTM, and Extended Tracking are some of the features of Vuforia SDK. Vuforia supports the detection of several kinds of targets (e.g. objects, images, English text). This'll play a big role in the execution of our idea. For more info, checkout Their Website.
