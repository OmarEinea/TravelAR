TravelAR App Demo

This interactive demo shows the most important current features of our app. It'll allow you to try it out before you install it.

Need a walkthrough?
  1. To start the demo, press "Tap to Demo" button
  2. Choose the airport you like
  3. Now you could try one of the following:
    1. Choose your airline then "Navigate to Check-in Counter"
    2. Enter your ticket number then "Navigate to Flight Gate"
    3. Go to "Facilites" tab and choose what you want then click the arrow to navigate to it
    4. Go to "Translate" tab and choose your language then "Enable Sign Translation" (This'll take effect in all navigations)
    5. In the "Translate" tab, you could read the airport announcements which are relevant to you
